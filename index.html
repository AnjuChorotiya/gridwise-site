[ 04 ] Process

Five steps. Two weeks. Shipped.

A repeatable sprint structure refined over dozens of SaaS engagements. No mystery, no agency theatre.

DAY 1
Audit

We review your current product, identify the highest-impact UI gaps, and define what's in scope. You get a written audit on day one — not week three.

DAY 2
Define

Sharp scope. What ships in two weeks, what doesn't. One Notion doc, one Figma file, one Slack channel. No kickoff workshop.

DAYS 3—8
Design

Daily Figma updates. Async Loom walkthroughs each evening. No scheduled review meetings unless decisions need them.

DAYS 9—11
System

Components extracted, tokens defined, documentation written. The work doesn't ship as flat screens — it ships as a living system.

DAYS 12—14
Handoff

Engineering-ready Figma + Loom walkthroughs per flow. Your engineers know exactly what to build before the sprint ends.

[ 06 ] FAQ

Common questions.

Do you work with pre-revenue startups? +

Yes — pre-seed and seed are our sweet spot. If you're funded (even a small angel round) and have a product live, we're a fit. We don't currently take on idea-stage projects with no MVP.

How fast can you start? +

Most sprints kick off within 5—7 business days of signing. We typically have one open slot in the current month and one in the following month.

Do you do branding, logos, or marketing sites? +

No. We focus exclusively on product UI and design systems for SaaS & AI startups. If you need a brand identity or marketing site, we'll happily refer you to studios we trust.

Do you work in Figma? How does handoff work? +

Yes — Figma is our primary tool. Handoff includes a structured Figma file, design tokens (Tailwind/CSS-ready), component documentation, and a Loom walkthrough per major flow. Your engineers know exactly what to build.

How does payment and contracting work? +

Standard MSA + SOW. Sprints are paid 50% on signing, 50% on delivery. Partner retainers are billed monthly in advance. We accept ACH, wire, and Stripe.

What if we need ongoing work after a sprint? +

Most sprint clients roll into a Partner retainer. We'll discuss it in week two — no pressure, no churn-or-lose-us tactics.

Can you sign an NDA? +

Of course. Send yours, or we can use ours. Standard mutual NDAs signed within 24 hours.
